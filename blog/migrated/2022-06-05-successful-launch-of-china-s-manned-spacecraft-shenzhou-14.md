今晨北京时间10:44分，载着两名男性航天员和一名女性航天员的神州十四号载人飞船，从酒泉载人飞船发射基地成功发射。三名航天员将在轨工作和生活六个月时间，于12月返回地球。

看着中国载人飞船如此频繁的发射，感叹国家对于航天技术之重视，以及投入之重大。当地球上的人类，正在经历着一个老牌帝国的没落和一个新兴超级力量的崛起、各国经济纷纷进入衰退周期困难重重之时，中国的航天工程依然可以有条不紊地按照既定的规划一步步的坚实推进，实在体现了这个国家要在太空探索上努力追赶奋勇当先的坚定决心。

去年六月的那次发射，6.5小时从发射到对接的速度，让太空旅行的时间长度一下子缩短到高铁旅行一般，那种惊艳至今难忘。不晓得一年之后的今天，在对接速度上，中国航天工程师们有没有进一步震惊世界的表现？拭目以待！

Beijing Time 10:44 am, Shenzhou-14 Manned Spacecraft with two male and one female Taikonauts successfully launched from Jiuquan Manned Spacecraft Launch Center. Three Taikonauts will work and live in China Space Station for six months and come back in December this year. 

Seeing so frequent launches of China's human-crewed spacecraft, you will definitely understand how vital the Space Program is for China and how much money and resources are paid in this field. While humans on the earth are experiencing the sunset of the old empire and the raising of the new superpower, economies of all countries are about to step into the Great Recession in the 21st century and all of them are having difficulties in all kinds of ways, China's Space Program is still going on steadily without disturbed by those obstacles. It reflects the country's firm determination to catch up and take the lead in space exploration.

Recalling the launch of last June, 6.5 hours from launch to docking shocked everyone in the worldwide range because it greatly reduce the time span of a space trip to that of a high-speed railway level. No one knows if Chinese space engineers are going to give the world a new surprise on the speed or not. We will see soon.
